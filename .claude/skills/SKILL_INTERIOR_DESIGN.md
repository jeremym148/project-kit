# Skill: Design Intérieur (Interior Design)

## Purpose
Interior design expertise for furniture layout, material selection, color palettes, ergonomics, and room styling. Practical guidelines for creating functional, aesthetically pleasing living spaces.

## Furniture Layout Principles

### Traffic flow (circulation)
- Main passage: min **0.90m** (1.20m in séjour)
- Between furniture pieces: min **0.60m**
- Around dining table: **0.75m** clearance for chairs
- In front of canapé: **0.45m** minimum to coffee table
- Behind desk chair: **0.75m** for push-back

### Focal points
- Each room should have one focal point (window, fireplace, TV wall, artwork)
- Arrange main furniture facing the focal point
- Séjour: canapé faces TV or fireplace, perpendicular to main window

### Symmetry vs asymmetry
- Formal rooms (dining): prefer symmetry
- Living spaces: slight asymmetry feels more natural and lived-in
- Balance visual weight (dark/heavy piece on one side = two lighter pieces on other)

### Séjour / Salon

|-----------|-------|-------|---------------|
| Canapé 2 places | 1.50–1.80m | 0.85–0.95m | 0.45m front |
| Canapé 3 places | 2.00–2.40m | 0.85–0.95m | 0.45m front |
| Canapé d'angle | 2.50–3.00m | 2.00–2.50m | 0.45m front |
| Table basse | 1.00–1.30m | 0.50–0.70m | 0.45m all sides |
| Meuble TV | 1.20–1.80m | 0.40–0.50m | 2.50m from canapé |
| Bibliothèque | 0.80–2.00m | 0.30–0.40m | against wall |
| Fauteuil (`armchair`) | 0.75–0.85m | 0.80–0.90m | 0.45m front |

#### Layout patterns
- **L-shape**: Canapé + fauteuil at 90° — good for conversation
- **U-shape**: Canapé + 2 fauteuils — formal, symmetrical
- **Face-à-face**: Two canapés facing — social, elegant
- TV distance: **2.5–3.5m** from screen to seating

- Bed headboard against a solid wall (not under window)

- Armoire near entrance for easy access

Three key points: **évier — plaque de cuisson — réfrigérateur**
- Each side: **1.20–2.70m**
- Total perimeter: **4.00–7.80m**
- No obstacle should cross the triangle

## Floor Materials Guide

### Parquet
- **Where**: Séjour, chambres, couloir, bureau, entrée
- **Look**: Warm, natural, elegant
- **Types**: Chêne massif (oak), contrecollé (engineered), stratifié (laminate)
- **Colors**: Chêne clair (light oak), noyer (walnut), wengé (dark)
- **3D material**: Warm brown tone, medium roughness (0.55)

### Carrelage
- **Where**: Cuisine, SdB, WC, entrée, buanderie, terrasse couverte
- **Look**: Clean, practical, modern or classic
- **Types**: Grès cérame, faïence, terre cuite, mosaïque
- **Sizes**: 30×30, 45×45, 60×60, 120×60 (grand format)
- **Colors**: Blanc, gris clair, gris foncé, beige, imitation bois/pierre
- **3D material**: Cool gray tone, low roughness (0.3)

### Pelouse
- **Where**: Jardin, terrasse extérieure, balcon (gazon synthétique)
- **Look**: Natural, fresh, outdoor living
- **3D material**: Green tone, high roughness (0.9)

### Material transitions
- Use a **barre de seuil** (threshold strip) between different floor materials
- Parquet → carrelage transitions typically at room doorways
- Keep the same material within a single room

## Lighting Design (for future features)

### Three layers of lighting
1. **Éclairage général** (ambient): Plafonnier, spots encastrés — 300 lux séjour, 150 lux chambre
2. **Éclairage fonctionnel** (task): Lampe de bureau, spots cuisine — 500 lux plan de travail
3. **Éclairage d'ambiance** (accent): Lampes à poser, rubans LED — indirect, warm (2700K)

### By room
| Room | General | Task | Accent |
|------|---------|------|--------|
| Séjour | Suspension/plafonnier | Liseuse | Lampes à poser |
| Chambre | Plafonnier dimmable | Chevet | Indirect mural |
| Cuisine | Spots encastrés | Sous-meubles hauts | Ilot suspension |
| SdB | Spots IP44 | Miroir éclairant | — |
| Bureau | Plafonnier | Lampe de bureau | — |

## Implementation Notes for the App

### Furniture placement assistance
- When placing furniture, respect minimum clearances from walls and other furniture
- App furniture types map to real dimensions:
  - `bed` → 1.60 × 2.00m (lit double 160)
  - `armchair` → 0.80 × 0.80m
  - `table` → 1.40 × 0.80m (table repas 4 pers.)
  - `chair` → 0.45 × 0.45m
  - `toilet` → 0.40 × 0.65m
  - `shower` → 0.90 × 0.90m
  - `bathtub` → 1.70 × 0.75m
  - `kitchen-counter` → 2.00 × 0.60m
  - `bathroom-cabinet` → 0.80 × 0.45m
  - `bookshelf` → 0.80 × 0.30m
  - `plant` → 0.40 × 0.40m (decorative)
  - `cabinet` → 1.00 × 0.60m (armoire)
  - `fridge` → 0.60 × 0.65m

### Auto-suggest floor material
Based on room label name:
```
Salon, Chambre, Couloir, Bureau, Entrée → parquet
Cuisine, SdB, Salle de bain, WC, Buanderie → carrelage
Terrasse, Balcon, Jardin → pelouse
```

### Room completeness check
Suggest missing furniture based on room type:
- **Chambre**: needs `bed` + `cabinet`
- **SdB**: needs `shower` or `bathtub` + `bathroom-cabinet`
- **WC**: needs `toilet`
- **Cuisine**: needs `kitchen-counter` + `fridge`
- **Salon**: needs `armchair` or suggest adding canapé furniture type
- **Bureau**: needs `table` + `chair` + `bookshelf`

### Style consistency
- All furniture in a room should match the same design style
- Floor material should be consistent within zones (all bedrooms same parquet, all wet rooms same carrelage)
- Suggest material when user creates a new room label based on room name
